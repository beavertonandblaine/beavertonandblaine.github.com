What is Beaverton & Blaine?

Beaverton & Blaine is a company devoted to bringing new life to discarded wood products.

Who is Beaverton & Blaine?

Beaverton & Blaine is a collaboration by Zeke Markshausen and Brandon Williams, both graduates of Northwestern University.

Where is Beaverton & Blaine?
